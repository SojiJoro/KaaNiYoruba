# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming space for anyone interested in
Yorùbá language technology, regardless of experience level, background, or
how fluent they are in Yorùbá. Corrections from learners and experts alike
are valued.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Assume good faith — language corrections are easy to get wrong, and that's
  fine; that's what review is for.
- Disagree about Yorùbá forms by citing sources, not by asserting authority.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language or jokes.
- Dismissing contributions because of someone's accent, dialect, or learner
  status.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the
maintainer directly. Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or
block contributors who violate this code of conduct.

This document is adapted from the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), kept short
for a small project.
